Dawat-e-Islami introduction

Dawat-e-Islami uses various mediums such as literature, online resources, and organized events to reach out to Muslims worldwide. It focuses on fostering a deeper understanding of Islamic principles and practices among its followers. The organization promotes peace, tolerance, and unity among Muslims, advocating a balanced approach to religious observance in contemporary society. "Dawat-e-Islami" is an Islamic organization founded in Pakistan in 1981 by Maulana Muhammad Ilyas Attar Qadri. It is known for its efforts in Islamic preaching, education, and social welfare. The organization emphasizes the propagation of Sunni Islam according to the Hanafi school of thought.

Propagation of Islam:

Educational Initiatives:

  • The organization provides educational resources and programs to enhance religious knowledge among Muslims. This includes publishing books, pamphlets, and online content that cover a wide range of Islamic topics.


Islamic Outreach and Education:

  • This involves organizing events, seminars, and workshops to educate both Muslims and non-Muslims about Islamic beliefs, practices, and values.


Community Service:

Media and Publications:

Dawat-e-Islami is known for its adherence to Sunni Islam and its emphasis on following the teachings of the Quran and Sunnah (traditions of the Prophet Muhammad).
